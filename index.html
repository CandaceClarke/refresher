Howdy do, how are you?

  • Good!
  • I'm alright.
  • Oh, you know.
  • Been better.
  • Not so great.

People love to ask other people how they are doing. There are lots of different ways to ask these questions. There may be even more ways to respond! Sometimes it's polite to give a quick and relatively upbeat answer, but you can always be honest with friends.
